about us

The story began in the 1990s when five friends with a shared passion for music decided to create a band that would push the boundaries of rock and soul. Tyler, a virtuoso on the bass guitar, provided the band's rock-solid foundation. Carson, with his electrifying guitar skills, added the searing riffs and soulful melodies that would become Kangaroo Kourt's signature sound. Mikey's dynamic drumming brought an irresistible groove, while Tate's powerful vocals and charismatic stage presence elevated the band to a whole new level. Ben, the keyboard maestro, contributed lush textures and soulful harmonies that completed the ensemble.

Kangaroo Kourt spent countless hours honing their craft, drawing inspiration from the rich musical history of Indiana and beyond. They seamlessly blended the raw energy of rock with the emotional depth of soul, creating a sound that was uniquely their own. Their lyrics were introspective and thought-provoking, touching on themes of love, loss, and the human experience.
